PGD

candidate applying for postgraduate diploma program (PGD) porgramme must posses a HND Upper Credit or above and five credits at 'O' levels in the relevant science subject. Applicants with professional certificates such as iCAN,ACA,FIST,FIMT,FIMT,FMLT,FNIST and other relevant qualifications may alsoqualify for admission.

  • A Bachalor Degree in the relevant discipline from FUTO or any other recognized University.

M.Sc,M.Eng,MPH

A first degree with at least a second honours qualification in the related area from futo or any other recognized university.

A bachalor degree from a recognized university plus a PGD in the relevant area.
